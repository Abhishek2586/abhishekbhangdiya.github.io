// 01 — about

          
The person behind the commits

        


        

          

            

              I'm a third-year B.Tech AI & ML student at Vishwakarma Institute of
              Technology, Pune (2024–2028), building systems that sit at the intersection
              of healthcare, computer vision, and agentic AI.
            

            

              My work follows a simple loop: take messy real-world input — a handwritten
              prescription, a CT scan, a sprawling cloud bill — and turn it into something
              a person or system can act on. That loop has produced a published paper,
              a 48-hour hackathon build, a patent application, and a few apps real people use.
            

            

              Outside the lab, I help run IEEE CIS as
              Management Secretary and coordinate operations for the
              GedIT Coding Club — mostly an excuse to drag
              more people into hackathons.
